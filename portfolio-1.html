Mastering Spanish verb conjugations can be challenging due to the complexity of the language. Verbs may sound similar across various tenses and modes, making memorization difficult. Traditional learning apps often mimic textbook methods, which might not suit everyone's learning style. This project was born out of a personal need to learn Spanish conjugations organically, through sentences and context. [ Read more >> ]
